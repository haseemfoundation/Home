Our Services

We provide specialized, child-focused services to nurture communication, learning, and emotional development. Our tailored programs are designed to address each child's unique needs, ensuring holistic growth and success. Together, we create a pathway to brighter futures for every child.

Comprehensive Assessment

We believe in understanding each child holistically before starting their therapy journey.

Our comprehensive assessments evaluate mental, physical, social, and educational abilities to identify their unique strengths and areas for development. This initial step ensures tailored strategies that meet their individual needs.

Speech Therapy

Effective communication opens doors to limitless possibilities. At Haseem Foundation & Academy, we offer individualized speech therapy sessions that focus on articulation, pronunciation, and comprehension.

These engaging, child-centered activities help children build the confidence they need to express themselves.

Behavioral and Emotional Management

Children sometimes struggle with emotions or behaviors that can hold them back. Our behavioral and emotional management programs use positive reinforcement, mindfulness techniques, and structured guidance to help children develop coping skills and emotional resilience.

Art and Play Therapy

Through creativity and play, children discover new ways to express themselves.

Our art and play therapy sessions provide a fun, safe space for children to explore their emotions while enhancing their imagination, social skills, and self-confidence.

Individualized Education Plans (IEPs)

Every child is unique, and their education should reflect that. Our IEPs are crafted to meet each child's specific learning and developmental goals, combining academic support with structured guidance to help them thrive in and out of the classroom.

Why Choose Haseem Foundation & Academy?

Our approach is built on compassion, expertise, and a deep commitment to your child’s success. From initial assessments to personalized therapy and skill-building sessions, we are with you every step of the way, creating a brighter, more independent future for your child.
